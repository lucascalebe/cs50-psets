Overview

I really love music, that's why i made a single page for it.

I used to like rock'n roll because of my dad's influence, but when i joined in the Music School i found other genres like jazz,bossa nova, r&b, and i fell in love for these genres. After sometime i got the first place in the contest to join in Professional guitarrist course in Music School of Brasilia. And since then i keep studying, but now i want to spend my time improving my programming skills.
